• Observables are lazy, promises are eagger meaning Observables are not executed until we subscribe to them using the subscribe() method whereas Promises executes immediately after its creation.
  • Observables Emit multiple values over a period of time whereas Promises Emit a single value at a time
  • Subscription of Observables can be cancelled, but promises subscription cannot be cancelled
  • Promises are always asynchronous but Observable can be synchronous or asynchronous
